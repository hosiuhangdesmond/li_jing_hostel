Room Pricing Information

At LI JING Hostel, we offer four distinct room types to suit your travel needs. Our Office-Style and Study-Focused rooms are available at HK$499 per day, ideal for business travelers and students seeking a productive environment. For those planning a romantic getaway or a family vacation, our Romantic-Style and Family-Friendly rooms are priced at HK$999 per day, offering spacious comfort and thoughtful amenities.

Please note that the above rates apply to room rental only. In addition, each guest or occupant—regardless of age, gender, or other personal attributes—is charged a fixed rate of HK$299 per day. This ensures fair and consistent service across all bookings.

Charges for rental services such as notebooks, computer accessories, phone chargers, and other optional items are not included in the room or guest fees. These services are available upon request and billed separately.

We encourage guests to review all pricing details before booking to ensure a smooth and transparent experience. LI JING Hostel is committed to providing affordable, high-quality accommodation for every traveler.

房價資訊

LI JING Hostel 提供四種房型，滿足不同旅客的需要。辦公室型及專注型房間每日租金為 HK$499，適合商務人士及學生使用。 浪漫型及家庭型房間則每日收費 HK$999，空間寬敞，設施貼心，適合情侶或家庭入住。

上述價格僅包括房間租金。另每位住客（無論年齡、性別或其他個人特徵）均需支付 每日 HK$299 的固定費用，以確保服務公平一致。

此外，租借服務（如筆記型電腦、電腦配件、手機充電器等）並不包含在房租或住客費用內。此類服務可按需提供，並另行收費。

我們建議旅客在預訂前詳細了解所有收費項目，以確保住宿過程順暢透明。LI JING Hostel 致力為每位旅客提供高質素且可負擔的住宿選擇。
