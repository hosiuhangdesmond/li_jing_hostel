LI JING Hostel is committed to safeguarding the privacy of our guests and complying with the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) of the Hong Kong SAR. As a non-profit hostel organization serving local and overseas travelers, we collect personal data solely for operational purposes such as booking, check-in, rental services, and travel assistance. All information is handled with strict confidentiality and used only for the intended purpose. We do not disclose personal data to third parties without consent, unless required by law. Guests have the right to access and correct their personal data held by us. By using our services, you agree to our privacy practices. For details, please refer to our full Privacy Policy or contact our customer service team.
